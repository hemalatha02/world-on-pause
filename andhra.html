The first case of the COVID-19 pandemic in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh was reported in Nellore on 12 March 2020. A 24-year-old who was confirmed positive for coronavirus, was also its first victim. He had travel history to Italy.[1] The Andhra Pradesh Health department has confirmed a total of 64,713 cases, including 823 deaths and 32,127 recoveries, as of 22 July.[2] The virus has spread in 13 districts of the state, of which East Godavari has the highest number of cases.
